Meet the founder


Hi there! I am Himanshu Seervi, the founder of HFlow. A little bit about me: I'm a university student studying software engineering at Guelph University. I love to play and watch sports, especially soccer; Big Fc Barcelona fan, right here.


Anyways, I got the idea to start HFlow when I was running my own marketing agency in high school and didn't have time to work on growing my business. I didn't have the budget to hire someone to do some of the tasks for me. So, I started to automate small tasks like sending cold emails and even an IG DM bot that would reply to any messages I received.
